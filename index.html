Our products & solutions

Over last one decade at Acceleron we have developed multiple products and solutions which can be deployed to group companies as well as external customers.

Vanijya (E commerce)

Vanijya is an E Commerce mobile app to serve retail customers of Heavy Engineering Equipment's. Customers can easily select parts, opt for desired delivery option, conveniently choose offers provided by the company and track status of orders

FSM (Field Service Management)

FSM is a Field Service Management mobile app which helps customers to raise service tickets. The technicians can perform their activities by updating status and using the artificially intelligent knowledge content to provide quality service by reducing resolution time significantly

LMS (Lead Management System)

LMS is a lead management system mobile app which helps sales team to capture and manage leads. The application can be interfaced with ERP like SAP to complete the journey of sales funnel by converting opportunities to quote and subsequent invoicing
